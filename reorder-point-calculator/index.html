Reorder Point Calculator

The reorder point tells you when to place a new order so you do not run out of stock. Enter your average daily demand, supplier lead time, and safety stock.

Reorder point: —

Formula

Reorder Point = (Daily Demand × Lead Time) + Safety Stock

Example

If you sell 20 units per day, your lead time is 10 days, and you keep 50 units as safety stock:

(20 × 10) + 50 = 250 units
